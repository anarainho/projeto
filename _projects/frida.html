FRIDA KHALO

Work developed on University

THE CHALLENGE

The purpose of this project was to design an interface using the Design System created during the classes of Interface Design.

That being said, we had to choose a physical product and design an online store – for desktop and mobile – that would only the selected product.

My choice was “Frida”, an illustrated book by Benjamin Lacombe (about some of the most relevant moments of Frida Kahlo’s life). For the final product, we should present three pages: Homepage, Product Details and Checkout.
